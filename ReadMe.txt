Fund Manager C# library

Assumptions made

Fund can hold more than one stock positions
Each stock in the fund is unique
Fund only have one cash position
Price stored in the stock position reflects the current market price of the stock
All Notional values are rounded to 2 decimal places
Cash will always be stored in pennies

Library Features

1) Ability to setup a new Fund
2) Ability to add stock and cash to the fund
3) Ability to configure benchmark index data
4) Calculate stock valuation with in the fund
5) Calculate cash position held in the fund
6) Calculate total fund value
7) Calculate weightage of stock in the fund
8) Compare stock weightage to benchmark index

Future changes
